Got Questions?

FAQ

Who can join AI Student Chapters? ▼
Any student at RCPIMRD, from MCA and IMCA (1st year), can apply. We welcome students from all technical and non-technical backgrounds who are passionate about AI.
Is there any fee to join? ▼
No, there is absolutely no fee required to become a member or participate in our events. Everything is free and open to students.
Do I need to know coding to join? ▼
Not necessarily! We have roles for designers, speakers, content creators, and event managers too. We'll help you learn coding if you're interested.
How do I register for Code-Carnival? ▼
Click the "Register Now" button in the Event section and fill out the Google Form, but spots are limited so register early!
